# About DICT

The Digital & Intelligence Convergence Team (DICT) focuses on building next-generation intelligent systems that integrate data, models, and decision-making processes.

---

## Vision

We aim to bridge **digital intelligence and real-world systems**, enabling AI to move beyond perception into structured reasoning and decision intelligence.

---

## Research Areas

- AI Systems Architecture  
- Multimodal Learning  
- Agent-based Intelligence  
- Scientific AI Infrastructure
